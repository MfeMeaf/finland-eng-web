Tourism in Finland

Helsinki

Helsinki is the capital city of Finland with a population of 1.3 million people, there are alot of things you can do in Helsinki such as going around the docks and visiting one of many food stalls they usually have open during the summer.


The ice cream you can find in Helsinki is also something worth checking out since they give you a lot for a good price and by a lot I mean ice cream as large or even larger than your fist.

Kemi

Kemi is a city in Lappland that has a snow castle resort where you can go to a cafe built out of snow or rent a room as well as those things there's art made out of snow as well. This is open all year round as it is often cold and easy to maintain the snow or ice all year.


Kemi is a prime place to go if you enjoy the cold. There is also a ski resort in kemi called Kalli ski resort.

Rovaniemi

Rovaniemi the capital of Lappland is also called the hometown of Santa Claus which means that you can visit Santa all year round with more than 500 thousand visitors annually, this shows that it’s popular among others.


This is also a place that offers different forms of enjoyment such as cafes and restaurants which means that you can bring along your family and enjoy some food together.
