The cultures in Finland

In this page we will talk about the cultures there are in Finland, and what makes them unique.


Background of the culture

Finnish culture is a mix between the indigenous heritage. Some examples would be the native languages of Finland, these being the Finnish and the Swedish languages, finnish being a finnic-uralic language and swedish being a germanic language. One example of something belonging to finnish culture is the Sauna, which is also a common cultural aspect in other nordic countries.
